Pricing, Custom Options & Ordering

We put the greatest attention and dedication to every detail. All our models have excellent electronics from Aguilar, Trickfish, Bartolini, Lindy Fralin or EMG pickups and preamps, the undisputed hardware of Hipshot and accessories from DRStrings, Daddario, Dunlop USA, our specially selected woods so that every Deeper has the guarantee of maximum excellence. We would like to help and advise you in the construction of your unique bass, look for the best option in combinations of woods and electronics so that your Deeper has a special audio, we also want your Deeper has the comfort you never find in other standard instruments.
